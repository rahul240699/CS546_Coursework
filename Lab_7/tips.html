Travel Tips and Tricks

  1. Stay Hydrated

    Make sure you are hydrated enough, as getting water might be tricky.

  2. Be Ready to walk

    While visiting these places, you might have to walk atleast 20000 steps a day, so prepare yourself.

  3. Eat Hygenic

    Make sure you eat at a place where hygiene is taken care of, as you might suffer Delhi Belly.
